SeaWorld Orlando | Orca Stadium OS

A secret training facility for animals, with a dark secret hidden behind it. The facility has a nuclear system in case they accidentally create a Biohazard event that could end the world. Little did they know, the system had a fatal flaw. The button on the wall that is supposed to restart the power grid to fix any issues, but.. they haven't done any testing or maintenance on it in decades. Leading to a massive world nuclear explosion that effected the land up to 40 miles, with nothing left. Can you figure out the mystery?
